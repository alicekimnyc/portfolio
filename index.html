About Me

If you hold a genuine belief that there’s a solution to every problem, then you will most likely find the solution. I’m a designer who is curious, likes to explore and creatively solve problems.

As a former Fashion Designer, my role was to design clothing that people can wear to look good and feel their best. Then I found myself interested in human behaviors. My passion is to enhance people's daily lives and contribute my skills in society by providing user-centered digital products.
